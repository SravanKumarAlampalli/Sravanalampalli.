Business Analysis Toolkit

A collection of analytical practices and methods I use to transform complex healthcare and insurance requirements into actionable, value-driven solutions.

My Approach

I use Agile-based analysis to define business objectives, elicit clear requirements, and ensure stakeholder alignment across multi-functional teams. My process integrates workshops, JAD sessions, and backlog grooming to reduce ambiguity and rework.

Key Techniques

  • Requirement documentation: BRD, FRD, SRS, Use Cases, User Stories
  • Analysis frameworks: GAP, Impact, SWOT, Risk, and Root Cause
  • Traceability: RTM and acceptance criteria mapping
  • Tools: Jira, Confluence, MS Project, and Figma for collaboration

Outcome

My structured documentation and stakeholder alignment methods have reduced project rework by up to 15% and improved sprint predictability across healthcare and insurance projects.
