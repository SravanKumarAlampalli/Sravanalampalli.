Healthcare Dashboards that Create Compliance & Command

A dashboard is only valuable if it changes behavior. I design Tableau/Power BI experiences that give leaders a single source of truth on claims timeliness, compliance posture, and member outcomes—so they can intervene early, allocate resources precisely, and demonstrate control to regulators and boards.

Design Principles I Use

  • Executive signal over noise: prioritized KPIs (SLA, denial drivers, backlog health) with drill-downs by line of business, region, and provider.
  • Operational alignment: views map to real teams and cadences (daily standups → weekly ops → month-end compliance).
  • Trust & repeatability: KPI definitions documented and tied back to SQL logic for auditability.

Outcomes Delivered

  • 20+ executive-level healthcare dashboards delivered on DataBricks-backed datasets.
  • 25% reduction in manual reporting time and higher confidence in compliance submissions.
  • Faster, more accurate decisions across claims, member experience, and regulatory reporting.

Where This Helps Immediately

Compliance reporting, capitation/claims cost tracking, utilization review visibility, denial prevention programs, and operational readiness for audits and corrective action plans.
