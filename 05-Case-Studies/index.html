Case Studies: From Insight to Impact in Healthcare

Below are representative outcomes from my recent roles. Each example pairs disciplined analysis with pragmatic delivery, producing measurable improvements in efficiency, compliance confidence, and stakeholder satisfaction.

1) Claims Optimization — National Health Plan

Challenge: Redundant validation steps and unclear ownership slowed adjudication and elevated denial risk.
Approach: Conducted GAP/impact analysis across claims intake and member management; re-authored user stories with crisp acceptance criteria; created executive dashboards to monitor cycle time and SLA adherence.
Outcome: 25% efficiency gain in claims operations; rework down; escalations reduced; improved readiness for compliance audits.

2) Insurance Analytics — Policy & Claims Insights

Challenge: Leaders lacked trustworthy views of policy renewals and claims trends.
Approach: Built SQL models and BI KPIs with documented metric definitions; instituted validation queries and governance around logic changes; educated stakeholders on interpretation.
Outcome: 20% fewer reporting errors and 15% faster decision cycles; clearer prioritization for product and compliance teams.

3) Agile Enablement — Delivery Cadence & Predictability

Challenge: Work entered sprints ambiguously, creating churn and missed expectations.
Approach: Introduced story mapping, JAD pre-reads, and DoR/DoD standards; tightened feedback loops with QA and business.
Outcome: 20% productivity uplift and 15% less rework; consistent sprint finishes; stakeholders gained confidence in release plans.
